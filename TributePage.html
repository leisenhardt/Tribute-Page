Facts about Joe Biden

  • Joe Biden became the Senator of Delaware at the age of 29
  • Joe Biden remained the Senator of Delaware for 36 years
  • Joe Biden became the 47th vice president of the United States and helped reduce gun violence, addressed violence against women, and helped pass and oversaw the recovery act.
  • Joe Biden announced he would run for president in 4/24/2019
  • Joe Biden then proceeded to be elected the President of the United States in 2021

Joe Biden was born November 20, 1942 and is now the 46th president of the United States. He was born in Pennsylvania and moved to Delaware when he was ten. In 1972, he became the senator of Delaware. Biden was reelected to the Senate 6 times by the time he bacame Obama's vice president.
